Getting started

  1. Pick any name in the top-right — that's how you'll appear on the leaderboard.
  2. Open a deck and click Study ▶ to review the cards that are due today.
  3. For each card, try to recall the answer, then click Show answer and rate how well you knew it.
  4. Come back tomorrow! Reviewing every day grows your streak 🔥.
Tip: Build your own deck with the New deck form, then add cards one at a time — or paste a passage of notes and let AI Generate do it for you.
